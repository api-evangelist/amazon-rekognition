Amazon announced general availability of Web Search for Amazon Bedrock AgentCore, a managed connector that lets AI agents retrieve current web information without third-party search APIs, credential management, or custom parsing. The tool is backed by Amazon's own index spanning billions of documents refreshed within minutes, keeps queries within AWS infrastructure, is priced at $7 per 1,000 queries, and integrates with MCP-compatible frameworks like Strands and LangChain.
